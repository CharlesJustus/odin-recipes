Pizza Recipe

This simple brick-oven pizza recipe has been made famous by several well-known, Brooklyn wood-fire pizzerias. Best accompanied by ice-cold, pilsner-style lager beer.

Dough Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup warm water
  • 1 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • 1 cup cold water
  • 3 cups bread flour
  • 1 tablespoon extr-virgin olive oil
`

Toppings Ingredients

  • 6 ounces low moisture mozzerla
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepeper
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 6 leaves fresh basil, torn

Directions

  1. Make the dough
  2. Knead dough
  3. Divide and rest dough
  4. Prepare dough
  5. Pre-heat oven
  6. Place toppings
  7. Bake pizza
