Burger Recipe

Ingredients

  • Homemade Burger Patty (see directions below)
  • Bun of your choice
  • Toppings of your choice
  • Optional: Cheese of your choice

Patty Ingredients

  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup fine dry bread crumbs

Instructions

  • Combine the patty ingredients then cook until well done
  • Assemble burger with your choice of bun and toppings.
